# Diva pet push-to-talk acceptance

## Acceptance steps

1. Open the desktop pet window.
2. Hold the floating mic button and speak.
3. Release the button.
4. Confirm the recognized text is sent to the current main chat.
5. Open the sidebar Diva pet view.
6. Hold the far-right push-to-talk mic button in the voice control row and speak.
7. Release the button.
8. Confirm the recognized text is sent through the same current chat flow.
9. Click the small plus button in the Whispers header.
10. Confirm the old topic is left in history, the current chat resets, and Diva says `让我们换个话题聊聊吧`.
