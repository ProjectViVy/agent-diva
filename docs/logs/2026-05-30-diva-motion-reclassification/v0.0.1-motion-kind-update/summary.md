# Summary

## Changes

- Reclassified `Relax`, `Sleepy`, and `LookAround` from idle motions to one-shot motions.
- Reclassified `show_full_body` from idle motion to startup motion.
- Updated the appearance startup motion selector to include every scanned startup motion.
- Allowed `show_full_body` to play through the avatar startup motion path.

## Impact

- Idle motion pools no longer include relax, sleepy, or look-around motions.
- Startup motion choices now include appearing, greeting, and full-body display.
- One-shot preview lists now include relax, sleepy, and look-around.
