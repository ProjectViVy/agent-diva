# Acceptance

## User-Facing Checks

- Open Diva pet role settings.
- Create or edit an appearance.
- Confirm the appearance editor shows a startup motion selector with `appearing` and `greeting`.
- Select `greeting`, save the appearance, and switch to that appearance.
- Confirm the avatar receives the selected startup motion on load or appearance switch.

## Animation Checks

- Open the animation panel.
- Confirm idle motions are listed separately from startup motions and one-shot motions.
- Preview `appearing` or `greeting`.
- Confirm one-shot motions remain previewable and are not shown as startup choices.
