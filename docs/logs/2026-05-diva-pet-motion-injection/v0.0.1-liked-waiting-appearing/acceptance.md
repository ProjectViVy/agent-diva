# Acceptance

## Steps

1. Open Diva Pet animation settings.
2. Confirm `waiting`, `appearing`, and `liked` are present in the available VRMA motion list.
3. Enable VRMA motion and include `waiting` in the selected idle motion IDs.
4. Preview or trigger `appearing` and `liked` as one-shot motions.

## Expected Result

- `waiting` can participate in idle motion playback.
- `appearing` and `liked` can be played as one-shot actions without breaking idle playback.
