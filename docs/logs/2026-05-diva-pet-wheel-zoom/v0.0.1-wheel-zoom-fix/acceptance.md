# Acceptance

1. Open the desktop pet window from the GUI.
2. Hover the cursor over the pet window and scroll up.
3. Confirm the avatar becomes larger.
4. Scroll down.
5. Confirm the avatar becomes smaller.
6. Enable click-through mode.
7. Confirm wheel scrolling no longer affects the pet scale.
8. Disable click-through mode, hold the drag handle, and move the avatar.
9. Confirm dragging still works and the avatar keeps the latest zoom level.
